Untitled Ninja Game

OH NO! The rival ninja clan has kidnapped the princess! They've taken her to the temple at the top of the mountain. How could this have happened!? It's up to you to save her before it's too late. Use your ninja reflexes and agility, and don't forget your trusty ninja star. When thrown, this magical shuriken always finds its way back to its owner.

Use your keyboard to control your ninja:
A: move left.
D: move right.
W: jump.
O: throw weapon left
P: throw weapon rigth
